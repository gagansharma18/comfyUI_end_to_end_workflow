# Cinematic Storyboard: The Restaurant Encounter

Prompt: A cozy modern five-star restaurant with glass windows overlooking a foggy pine forest, natural mountain light, summer evening. An ultra-wide HDRI image captured on a ricoh 360 degree camera.

## Characters
- Woman: a candid portrait of a woman with natural makeup showcasing her unique facial features. Path: input/characters/woman.jpg
- Man: a unique-looking fashion model with distinct facial features, close-up shot. Path: input/characters/man.jpg

## Shots

Prompt: The characters Woman and Man are at a table in the restaurant. They are sitting opposite of each other. A cinematic movie scene with professional lighting, medium shot.
Narration: Woman and Man met at the restaurant at sunset.

### Shot 2
Prompt: Next scene: The camera is behind the Man, creating an over-the-shoulder shot showing a frontal view of the Woman smiling. She is looking at him warmly.
Narration: She looked at him with a warm smile, breaking the tension.

### Shot 3
Prompt: Next scene: Close-up of the Man's face, he is surprised and happy. The restaurant background is softly blurred.
